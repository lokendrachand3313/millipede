Our Mission

Our mission is to gather millipedes from various geographical locations of Nepal and accurately identify them to species level. This involves field collection, dissection and identification, morphological and molecular analysis, specimen preservation, specimen documentation, and detailed analysis to enhance the understanding of millipede biodiversity in Nepal.

Why are we working on Millipedes?

Nepal is rich in Diplopod fauna. After compiling the checklist of millipedes, we found that the whole western and southern parts are unexplored. All the species identified on the basis of morphology. Integrative taxonomy may also discover new species from explored area as well as unexplored parts.
