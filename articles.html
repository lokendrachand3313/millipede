Trigoniulus corallinus - Live specimen in natural habitat
Diplopoda: Spirobolida: Pachybolidae

First Record of Spirobolid Trigoniulus corallinus (Gervais, 1842) from Nepal

In Prep Author: Chand, L. üì∏ Specimen Available
Order:Spirobolida
Family:Pachybolidae
Genus:Trigoniulus
Species:corallinus
Collected by:L. Chand & A. Senchury
Type Locality: Kathmandu Valley, Central Nepal (27.7172¬∞N, 85.3240¬∞E)

Abstract: This paper reports the first confirmed presence of the rusty millipede, Trigoniulus corallinus, in the urban environments of Nepal. Specimens were collected from the Kathmandu Valley, marking a significant range expansion for this anthropochore species. The record highlights the role of human activity in the dispersal of tropical millipedes into Himalayan regions.

Abstract: The Harpagophorid species Gonoplectus lindbergi is documented for the first time in Nepal. Previously known from Eastern Himalayan territories, these new sightings in the Central Himalayan belt provide valuable data for the biogeographical mapping of South Asian Myriapoda.
